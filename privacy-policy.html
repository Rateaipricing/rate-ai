Privacy Policy

Effective date: 1 April 2026

Overview

Rate AI ("we", "our", or "the app") is a pricing and task-management tool built for A-Team Electricians technicians. This policy explains what personal information we collect, why we collect it, and how it is protected. We do not sell, rent, or share your data with advertisers or third-party marketing services.

We do not collect location data, contacts, photos, payment card numbers, health data, browsing history, or any advertising identifiers.

How We Use Your Information

  • Authenticating you when you sign in
  • Displaying your name and role inside the app
  • Sending a password-reset email when you request one
  • Caching pricing data locally on your device for offline use

We use your data only to operate the app. We never use it for advertising, profiling, or any purpose beyond core app functionality.

Service Providers

We use the following third-party services to operate Rate AI:

  • Google Firebase (Authentication & Firestore database) — your email, name, and role are stored in Firebase on servers managed by Google. Firebase's privacy policy is available at firebase.google.com/support/privacy.

No other third-party services receive your personal data.

Data Storage & Security

  • Account data is stored in Google Firebase (EU/US data centres).
  • Pricing and cart data is cached locally on your device using encrypted AsyncStorage.
  • All data in transit is encrypted via HTTPS / TLS.
  • Access to the Firebase database is restricted by Firestore Security Rules — only authenticated users with a valid account can read their own data.

Data Retention & Deletion

Your account data is retained for as long as your account is active. To request deletion of your account and all associated data, email us at support@rateai.app. We will process your request within 30 days.

Children's Privacy

Rate AI is intended for use by adult electricians and tradespeople. We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone under the age of 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Object to or restrict processing of your data

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at support@rateai.app.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will revise the effective date at the top of this page. Continued use of the app after changes are posted constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
